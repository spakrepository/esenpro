The Brand 'Esenpro'

We are pleased to introduce to you “ESENPRO”, a brand of Essential Power Transmission Pvt. Ltd., India, established in 1947. Esenpro is a 3rd generation family business having its manufacturing unit in Gujarat and Head Quarters in Mumbai.

Esenpro is a leading manufacturer and exporter of Standard and Custom Built Industrial Gearboxes. We are an ISO 9001:2015 certified company by TUV SUD. With a wide product range, Esenpro has multiple installations globally in the steel, cement, plastic, mining, power, overhead cranes, port, and chemical industries.

The manufacturing unit is equipped with the state of the art CNC machines and other infrastructural equipment to produce high-quality gears. In-house manufacturing includes Shaft and Blank Turning, Gear Hobbing, Heat treatment, Cylindrical Grinding, Gear Teeth Profile Grinding.

Our team includes experienced technocrats and application engineers who are designing gearboxes to best suit the end-user application. By using the latest design software and simulation modules we are confident that Esenpro delivers gearboxes that are of the highest quality standard.

We have always given utmost priority to QUALITY, and that has been our drive to success.

We thank you for visiting our website and we look forward to working with you.

Vision

Esenpro continuously upgrades its designs & standards, which enables us to manufacture quality gearboxes. Esenpro employees are our prime assets, and motivating them is our key objective.

Mission

Esenpro provides end to end gearing solutions for varied applications in the engineering industry. Our dedication to Quality will pave the way to be the emerging leaders in the gearbox market.
